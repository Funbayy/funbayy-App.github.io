Frequently Asked Questions

Does Funbayy steal your data

No, Funbayy only collects necessary information for the usability of app and for security purposes.

Like Username and password for personal access and other optional info of email , phone_no for future addition of features.

All the data is stored locally in your phone for now.

What is the purpose of Funbayy

Nowadays almost every big production company is starting its own streaming service , because of which user are forced to pay for multiple streaming services.

Funbayy was created as a collection of all movies and tv shows from different streaming services into a single application free of cost.

Is Funbayy Legal?

Funbayy does not own or host any of the movies / Tvshows listed in this app. It fetches the information available on the internet and shows it in this app.

This app is not made for commercial use and was intended as a hobby project/for personal use.

I'm not able to watch some listed content

You may not be able to watch some of the listed content due to following reasons

1) The movie/Tvshow is too old and so is not available

2) Your Internet connection might be slow , Try refreshing the internet connection and reloading the content

3) The movie has not released/just released and is not available on any streaming service

4) The movie/Tvshow got taken down due to copyright violation from the hosting server

Why download option of whole season does not work ?

Currently there is no option for downloading whole season altogether as we ran into issues while implementing this feature.

We are working on this feature and maybe this feature will be available soon..

Why there is no search bar for searching anime ?

There was no need to implement an another search bar for anime as anime movies / Tvshows can be searched in Movies / Tvshows search bar respectively
